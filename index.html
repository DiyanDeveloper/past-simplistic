Quick Overview

The Past Simple is used to describe completed actions in the past.

  • Regular verbs: add -ed (play → played)
  • Irregular verbs: unique forms (go → went, eat → ate)
  • Common time expressions: yesterday, last night, in 2010

Examples:

"She visited Paris last summer."

"They didn't finish their homework."
